For much of 2020 now, Shelter-in-Place measures have remained active due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Having felt somewhat isolated in the midst of all of this, i've found that the thought of traveling and pictures of places far away have made me feel really good. With this blog i've decided to showcase three countries that i've most recently read about: Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, & Croatia.

Kazakhstan

Here is an aerial view with the Baiterek Tower in Nur-Sultan, the capital of Kazahkstan. Click to see images of Almaty, Aktoby, Issyk Lake, & Pavlodar.

Uzbekistan

This view shows the square that contains the statue of Amir Timur in Tashkent, the capital of Uzbekistan. Click to see images of Tashkent, Bukara, Itchan Kala, Khiva, & Samarkand.

Croatia
